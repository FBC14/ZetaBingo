How to play

  1. Open Zeta's stream. Each boxes inside of the bingo represents the action that Zeta or chat does during the stream.

  2. When Zeta or chat does an action exactly as the box describes, click on it to mark the box.

    The Bazo icon in the middle is a free-space.

  3. The objective of the game is to complete a specific pattern on the bingo table. The pattern can only be a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).

  4. Once you completed a straight line pattern, each letters on the "B I N G O" will be highlighted in gray.

  5. When all of the "B I N G O" letters are highlighted in grey, then you win the game!

  6. You can reset the bingo game by refreshing the page or clicking on the "RESET" button at the bottom
